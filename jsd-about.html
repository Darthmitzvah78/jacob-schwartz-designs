Background

I am passionate, driven, self-taught. An art director and designer from Birmingham, Alabama, with more than 16 years of experience. Prior to my career as a designer, I spent formative years in an apprenticeship that led to a job as a sushi chef at Birmingham's most treasured Japanese restaurant. Oddly enough, creating sushi profoundly influenced my eye for composition and harmony.

I also devoted much of my time to graffiti art — concepting murals, sketching plans and color schematics, hosting and collaborating with other painters for Writer's Block, an annual graffiti event sponsored by Red Bull. That work impressed upon me the importance and impact of considerate design and collaborative process.

Throughout my professional design years, I've cultivated a deftness for typography, lettering, logos, and branding. I enjoy designing with intent through strategic collaboration and problem solving. I believe in a client-centric teamwork approach paired with discipline, restraint, and a thorough understanding of design trends and history.

Great design doesn't arise from linear effort. It requires a dynamic journey — collaboration, research, more collaboration, refinement, coffee, more refinement. It defies process. And in the end, it must serve the client well. It must create success.
